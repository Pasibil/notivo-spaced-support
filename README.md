## About the App
Learn faster and remember longer with a smart spaced repetition system based on Hermann Ebbinghaus’ memory curve.  

This app helps you study effectively by scheduling your reviews at scientifically optimized intervals — just before you’re about to forget. Add your own words, phrases, or facts, and let the algorithm handle the timing for you. You can also set daily reminders to stay consistent and make steady progress.

## FAQ
- **How do I add study material?**  
  On the main page, simply enter your text and its meaning.  
  When you’re ready to save, tap the **+ button** that pulses above the keyboard in the bottom-right corner.  
  Your new item will be added to the memory, and you can start practicing right away.

- **How does spaced repetition work?**  
  The app uses Ebbinghaus’ memory curve to schedule reviews at optimal times — right before you’re likely to forget — so you retain information longer.

- **Can I track my progress?**  
  Yes, the app provides a detailed review history and progress tracking so you can monitor your learning over time.

- **Can I set reminders?**  
  Yes, you can enable optional daily reminders to stay consistent and keep up with your study routine.

- **Is my data private?**  
  All study material is stored locally on your device. No login is required, ensuring complete privacy.
